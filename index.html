ARE YOU READY TO JUMP

You will find your character standing at the bottom left of the screen. There will be multiple blocks zipping towards your character from the bottom right side of the screen. Soon as you feel like the blocks are close enough and your character is in danger of being hit press the UP arrow key and make your character JUMP and save him from the blocks. If your character dies DONT WORRY ,simply hit the Replay Button and start playing again.
